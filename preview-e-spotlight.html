Meet the witches, students, and hearts of Skygate, Willowhill, and beyond.

Yuyao

Taotao's Best Friend

High-spirited, fiercely loyal, and endlessly protective, Yuyao is the heart that keeps the group moving forward — through hot pot dinners, karaoke nights, and every magical mishap in between. Where Taotao hesitates, Yuyao charges ahead with a grin.

"Let's go celebrate!"

Wanwan

The Model Student

Elegant, graceful, and impossibly disciplined — Wanwan carries the weight of being "perfect" every single day. Her magic blooms as delicate orchids, but underneath the poise is a girl learning that imperfection isn't failure.

"That's not a bad thing either."

Zhu Bei

Top-Ranked Student

Quiet, humble, and easily flustered by attention, Zhu Bei would rather disappear into a game of Wizard's Ball than bask in the spotlight his grades earned him. He also happens to be hopelessly, quietly smitten with Wanwan.

"I don't like the attention."
