Cassidy

Acting Mayor of Westwitch

With curly red hair and a freckled face beneath her pointed hat, Cassidy takes her role as Westwitch's acting mayor extremely seriously — sometimes to the point of getting delightfully flustered under the pressure.

She believes that leading by example is the only way to bring her divided town back together, even when that means confronting an invisible barrier that everyone else has learned to ignore.

"As the daughter of the mayor, I'm obliged to uphold Westwitch's values and set a good example. If I sit back and do nothing, will my neighbors see that and do so as well?"
