Anran

A Heart That Just Wanted to Be Seen

For years, Anran was overlooked, dismissed, and quietly bullied — made to feel like he was never quite good enough, no matter how hard he tried. That old hurt never really healed; it just learned to hide.

Under the alias Alden Riddle, that hidden pain resurfaces as something powerful and unstable, pulling you and your friends into a mystery that isn't really about revenge at all — it's about being heard, at last.

"I worked hard, but my best was never good enough... All I've wanted all these years was an apology."
