Amalia

Westwitch Guide

Sophisticated and resilient, with a fashionable eye and a writer's mind, Amalia knows Westwitch better than anyone — including its best-kept secrets.

On her thirteenth birthday, an accidental curse made her invisible for an entire year. The experience was lonely, but it left her with a rare gift: the ability to see through illusions that fool everyone else.

"You could say that I can see invisible people too... I've been invisible before. On my thirteenth birthday, I accidentally cursed myself."
