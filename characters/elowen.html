Elowen

A Girl Learning to Change

Once the most popular girl at school, chestnut-haired Elowen wore only high-end fashion and never thought twice about who she stepped on to stay on top — including a quiet, easily overlooked classmate named Anran.

When her family's fortune collapsed almost overnight, Elowen learned exactly what it felt like to be the one everyone turns away from. It's a painful lesson, but one that finally teaches her to take responsibility for the hurt she caused.

"I went from being the most popular girl in school to the girl no one wants to associate with in a matter of days... I was immature and took it out on you."
