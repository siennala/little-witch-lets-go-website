Mudan

Tattoo Artist of Willowhill

Calm, focused, and deeply artistic, Mudan tattoos blooming peonies onto her own arm and magic ink onto everyone else's. Her studio glows in shades of green and magenta, warm and welcoming.

Her ink carries real protective magic — but she prefers to hide the message inside intricate designs rather than announce it outright, much like she keeps her own feelings close.

"There's magic in the paint, but it has to be paired with the art itself to work. I like to take it a step further and hide the message in intricate designs."
