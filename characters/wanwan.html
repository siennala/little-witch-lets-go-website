Wanwan

The Model Student

Elegant, graceful, and impossibly disciplined — Wanwan carries the weight of being "perfect" every single day. Her magic blooms as delicate orchids, but underneath the poise is a girl learning that imperfection isn't failure.

When Zhu Bei unexpectedly outranks her, the pressure she's carried her whole life finally starts to crack — and in that crack, something more honest begins to grow.

"No one can be perfect. As long as we're human, we'll always have our imperfections. And that's not a bad thing either."
