Zhu Bei

Top-Ranked Student

Quiet, humble, and easily flustered by attention, Zhu Bei would rather disappear into a game of Wizard's Ball than bask in the spotlight his grades earned him. He also happens to be hopelessly, quietly smitten with Wanwan.

When rumors swirl that he cheated his way to first place, you are one of the few who takes the time to actually get to know him — and finds a much gentler person than the rumors suggest.

"I've always gone to school here. I just like to try and keep a low profile. I don't like the attention."
