Faera

Musical Witch of Willowhill

Dreamy and whimsical, with lavender tucked into her pointed hat and a magical violin always close at hand, Faera fills Willowhill with music. She loves nothing more than an audience — and a little romance.

Her enchanted melodies are beautiful, but a spell gone slightly astray once put the whole town to sleep — a mishap you and Yuyao arrive just in time to help undo.

"Are you sure you're not just coming back for my music? It's so romantic, isn't it?"
