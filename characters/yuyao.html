Yuyao

Your Best Friend

High-spirited, fiercely loyal, and endlessly protective, Yuyao is the heart that keeps the group moving forward — through hot pot dinners, karaoke nights, and every magical mishap in between. Where you hesitate, Yuyao charges ahead with a grin.

She's your constant companion on the road to Wisteria City, and the first to defend her friends — whether from unfair rumors or from themselves.

"Let's go celebrate!"
