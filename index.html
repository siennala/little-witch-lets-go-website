The Story

Deep in the mountain town of Skygate, a young witch — you — sets off for the metropolitan Wisteria City to compete in a prestigious magic competition, with your energetic best friend Yuyao and loyal black cat Benben by your side.

Along the way, through the sleepy town of Willowhill, the divided streets of Westwitch, the enchanted forests of Mythodor, and the snowy corners of Hellridge, you and your friends untangle old rivalries, mend broken hearts, and slowly realize that a mysterious figure has been watching you all along.

There's laughter over shared meals, warm cups of tea between old and new friends, and the kind of bonds that make even the most faraway towns start to feel like home. In the end, it's not the flashiest spell that wins the day, but friendship, forgiveness, and the quiet courage to be imperfect.

"No one can be perfect. As long as we're human, we'll always have our imperfections. And that's not a bad thing either." — Wanwan
